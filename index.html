Big Bang

Welcome! The Universe is everything from the tiniest particles, to the largest galaxies to the very existance of space, time and life. "The Origin of the Universe is the Origin of everything". Multiple Scientific Theories plus creation myths from around the world, tries to explain its mysterious genesis. However most widely accepted explanation is the Big Bang Theory.

Radiation Era

What?

NAMED FOR DOMINANCE OF RADIATION

    This Radiation era is divided into small Epoches called (PLANCK,GRAND UNIFICATION,INFLATIONARY,ELECTROWEAK,QUARKS,HADRON,LEPTON,NEUCLEAR). This era is occured within the universe of first 1000 years.

Matter Era

What?

PRESENCE AND PREDOMINANCE OF MATTER IN THE UNIVERSE

This Matter era is the vast majority of the universelife span includes present day. There are 3 types of Epoches(ATOMIC,GALACTIC,STELLAR).

Epoches of Era

Epoches in Radiation Era

PLANCK EPOCHE

- NO MATTER EXISTED ONLY ENERGY OF THE SUPERFORCEEXISTS

GRAND UNIFICATION EPOCHE

- THE STRONG NEUCLEAR BROKE AWAY

INFLATIONARY EPOCHE

- DURING THIS EPOCHE THE UNIVERSE RAPIDLY EXPANDED.

ELECTROWEAK EPOCHE

- THERE IS A SPLIT OF STRONG AND WEAK ELECTROMAGNETIC FORCES.

QUARK EPOCHE

- ALL THE INGREDIANTS WERE PRESENT HOWEVER THE UNIVERSEIS STILL TOO HOT AND DENSE.

HADRON EPOCHE

- THE UNIVERSE COOLED DOWN ENOUGH FOR QUARKS TO BIND TO FORM PROTONS AND NEUTRONS.

LEPTON AND NEUCLEAR EPOCHE

- THE PROTONS AND NEUTRONS UNDERWENT SIGNIFICANT CHANGES THEY FUSED AND CREATED THE NUCLEI.

Epoches in Matter Era

ATOMIC EPOCHE

- THE UNIVERSE COOLED DOWN AND THE RECOMBINATION OF ELECTRON AND PROTONS TO FORM THE THE FIRST ELEMENT CALLED HYDROGEN(H).

GALACTIC EPOCHE

- THE CLOUDS ARE FORMED DURING THIS EPOCHE, IT HAS PACKETS OF GAS MAY HAVE HAD ENOUGH GRAVITY TO CAUSE ATOMS TO COLLECT.

STELLAR EPOCHE

- STARS BEGAN TO FORM, HERE THE CURRENT STAGE OF UNIVERSEIS DEVELOPED.
